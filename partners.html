Our Partners

Sprouts for Learning collaborates with global organizations, schools, and libraries to bring quality education and creative opportunities to children worldwide.

Learning Planet Alliance x UNESCO

The Learning Planet Alliance unites all change actors, from grassroots players and innovators to youth movements, national governments, and multilateral organizations. Together we co-create new ways of learning, teaching, and researching by sharing our data, practices, knowledge, and experience. The LearningPlanet Alliance and Sprouts for Learning enter into this partnership agreement with the shared mission to unite to transform education.

EmpowerED

EmpowerED is a student-led organization in South Korea focused on bringing quality education to all parts of the world. Sprouts for Learning works with EmpowerED to create pen pal programs between students in South Korea and Pakistan. Furthermore, Sprouts for Learning and EmpowerED work together to produce illustrated picture books for kids in 5+ nations.

Nanum Library

A private library in South Korea where Sprouts volunteers teach English through interactive sessions and storytelling.

Heavenly Dream School

Heavenly Dream School is a school for North Korean Defectors to learn basic literacy and numeracy skills. Sprouts for Learning helps provide the Heavenly Dream School with illustrated picture books and tailor-made worksheets to help supplement learning.

E&H Foundation

E&H Foundation is an Indian-based organization helping over 30,000+ kids attain quality education in the slums of Uttar Pradesh. Sprouts for Learning helps provide students with illustrated picture books and tailor-made worksheets to help kids supplement their learning.

Dazzling Stone Orphanage for Children

The Dazzling Stone Orphanage for Children is an orphanage located in Chennai, India. The orphanage has a school integrated into it. Sprouts for Learning helps provide students with illustrated picture books and tailor-made worksheet booklets to help kids supplement their learning.

Suwon City Library

Suwon City Library is a government-funded library in South Korea, with free learning programs for kids. Sprouts for Learning provides Suwon City Library with volunteers and worksheet booklets to help kids increase their English capabilities.

Dulwich College Suzhou

Sprouts for Learning is partnering with the service club at Dulwich College Suzhou. The service club focuses on providing quality educational materials to students in rural China. Sprouts for Learning works with the members at the service club to help create worksheets booklets, and lesson slides.

Smile for All

Smile for All is an Indian-based organization helping over 10,000+ kids attain quality education obtain education in rural India. Sprouts for Learning helps provide students with illustrated picture books and tailor-made worksheets to help kids supplement their learning.

The Pearl S. Buke Foundation

The Pearl S. Buck Foundation is a nonprofit organization founded in 1964, inspired by the author and Nobel laureate Pearl S. Buck. It aims to support underprivileged children, especially in Asia, through various educational, health, and social service programs. Sprouts for Learning is working with the Pearl S. Buck foundation to increase cultural awareness through social media campaigns and exhibitions!!

St. Philomena Nursery and Primary school-lwabikere

Sprouts for Learning provides St. Philomena Nursery and Primary school-lwabikere in Uganda with books and worksheet booklets to help kids increase their English capabilities. In addition, members of Sprouts for Learning are working on writing and illustrating more books to send to this school
