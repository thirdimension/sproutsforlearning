Executive Members

Founder – Dharshwana Muralidharan

Dharshwana Muralidharan is a sophomore at Gyeonggi Suwon International School. Dharshwana has always been an advocate for education, and she started volunteering for organizations promoting education such as Kalvi-40 and Teach for Speech in Grade 5. Through her volunteering experiences, she understood the importance of education and realized students require fun, innovative learning methods. Thus, during Grade 8, she started Sprouts for Learning. She has helped secure partnerships with local and international organizations to help bring quality education to all. Dharshwana hopes Sprouts for Learning can help many more in the future!

Educational Director: Raina Jeon

Raina Jeon is a STEM enthusiast, especially in the field of oncology and molecular biology. She is a strong advocate for the advancement of quality STEM education for students around the world. Currently, she helps plan and execute the curriculum for the students Sprouts for Learning is helping. In her free time, she enjoys playing badminton and hanging out with her friends.

Social Media Director – Hannah Yoon

Hannah Yoon is a passionate designer and artist. She enjoys using art as a medium to inspire and communicate powerful messages. Beyond her artistic pursuits, Hannah is a dedicated advocate for education, believing in its transformative power to change lives and create equal opportunities. She is particularly focused on ensuring that quality education is accessible to all, especially underserved communities. Her goal is to merge her love for design and her passion for education, using her talents to make a meaningful impact in both fields.
